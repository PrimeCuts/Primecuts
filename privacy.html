Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Prime Cuts Saloon, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our "Book Appointment" form or contact us, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Personal Identification: Full Name, Phone Number.
  • Appointment Details: Preferred service type (Haircut, Beard, Facial) and preferred dates.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information (collected automatically via standard web analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To schedule and confirm your appointments.
  • To contact you regarding updates or changes to your booking via Phone or WhatsApp.
  • To improve our website functionality and customer service experience.
  • To comply with local legal obligations in Pakistan.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services to enhance your experience. These services may have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Maps: Used to display our location. Google may collect location data and set cookies.
  • WhatsApp: When you click the WhatsApp button, you are redirected to the WhatsApp application. We do not store your chat history on our servers.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect how the website functions (e.g., Google Maps).
